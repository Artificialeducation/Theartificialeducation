About The Artificial Education

The Artificial Education is built with a simple goal — to make computer learning easy, practical, and
accessible for everyone. We explain concepts in Hinglish
so that every student can understand clearly. Our focus is not just on theory but on real skills through
practical projects, helping you become job-ready.

Hi, I'm Pawan Soni

Hi, I'm Pawan Soni from Rewari, Haryana. I am the founder of The Artificial EducationYouTube channel.

Earlier, I used to create roasting and comedy videos, but now my main focus is teaching computer skills to students from absolute zero level in a very simple and easy-to-understand way.

My courses are completely practical and step-by-step. I teach MS Office, Tally Prime, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C Language, and Python with real-life examples so that you can learn easily and apply these skills in jobs or your own business.
